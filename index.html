About CASTPLUS

Castplus was established in 2004.

We provide international investment casting and metal manufacturing services.

Our products include mechanical fittings, automotive fittings, construction hardware, medical equipment, valve fittings, sewing machines, knitting fittings, pneumatic tools and marine hardware.

We ship internationally from our base of operations in Jiulong Industrial Park, Jiulong Town, Jiaozhou City, Qingdao, Shandong Province, P.R. China.
